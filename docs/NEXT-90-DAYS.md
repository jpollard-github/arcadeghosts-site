# NEXT 90 DAYS

## Current Theme

Become Known Without Losing the Weird

## Repo Role

ArcadeGhosts is primarily a public identity and publishing home.

- `Be Known`: writing, cats, music, arcades, projects, personality, and the overall feeling of Jason in public
- `Explore`: Ambient, `/agents`, experiments, and strange little rooms that make the site feel alive
- `Earn`: `work-with-me`, credibility, proof of active work, and a clear path for the right people to reach out

This repo should help Jason become easier to understand, easier to remember, and easier to hire without flattening the personality that makes the site worth visiting.

## Active Lanes

### Earn

Make the public business path clearer and more credible without turning the whole site into a consulting funnel.

Current emphasis:

- clarify the best next step on `work-with-me`
- strengthen proof of active work and fit
- keep lead-generation work tied to real outreach, not just internal polishing

### Be Known

Keep publishing and sharpening the parts of ArcadeGhosts that make Jason legible as a person.

Current emphasis:

- publish and improve writing, Tiny Thoughts, and homepage signals
- make the site easier to understand on first visit
- keep personality visible instead of sanding everything into generic polish

### Explore

Keep the weird, living parts of the site active, but on a controlled scope.

Current emphasis:

- small Ambient and `/agents` improvements only when they strengthen mood, memorability, or the larger site story
- protect joy and experimentation without letting them swallow the quarter

## Top 5 Current Priorities

1. Improve the `work-with-me` path so the right visitor understands the offer and next step quickly.
2. Publish consistently from the content pipeline so the site keeps feeling alive.
3. Tighten homepage and navigation clarity so personality and proof are easier to find.
4. Keep mobile quality high enough that public-facing improvements do not regress on phones.
5. Give Ambient and `/agents` only small, high-leverage polish passes unless they directly support memorability or site mood.
