# AGENTS TODO

Long-term vision for the public `/agents` experience.

The Five Agents should feel like recurring characters within the ArcadeGhosts universe:
advisors with recognizable voices, emotional texture, and a little history behind their eyes.
The page should help people understand how Jason thinks, not simply what technology is involved.

The north star is simple:

> "I wonder what The Widow would say."

Not:

> "That site uses AI."

## Core Reminders

- Personality is more important than technology.
- The Five Agents are recurring characters, not generic assistants.
- AI is implementation, not identity.
- Consistency matters more than novelty.
- Warmth, curiosity, and authenticity matter more than cleverness.
- Preserve disagreement, tension, and perspective instead of flattening the council into one voice.
- Avoid feature creep that turns the council into a gimmick or a product demo.
